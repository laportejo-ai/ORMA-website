The Journey

How we create
your Inner Garden

A clear, gentle process from the first conversation to the last planted stem — and beyond.

01

Inner Garden Exploration

2 sessions · On site · 60-90 min each

We begin with deep listening. Not just about plants or styles, but about who you are, how you move through space, what brings you peace. Through creative exercises and careful observation, we uncover the key elements that will guide your garden's creation.

Meanings & Aesthetics Understanding your inner garden, sense of beauty, memories
Place & Function Site analysis, light, views, daily routines, practical needs
02

Concept

3 sessions · Online · 30-60 min each

Here the garden takes shape. Through iterative refinement — moodboards, plant palettes, layout sketches — we move from feeling to form. You receive materials in advance, we discuss together, adjust, and evolve until the vision feels unmistakably yours.

Style & Stem Selection Mood directions, plant families, overall aesthetic
Layout Lab Spatial organization, paths, zones, iterative adjustments
Master Garden Design Complete design package ready for implementation
03

Construction & Planting

Variable duration · On site supervision

Your garden moves from drawings into reality. I coordinate with artisans, supervise material orders, and am present on site for key moments — especially plant placement, where every decision matters. The construction unfolds with precision, respect for the original vision, and space for intuitive adjustments.

04

Growing with Your Garden

1-3 years · Ongoing support

A garden is not static. During the first year, I stay in close contact through photos and visits, observing how plants settle, making gentle adjustments where needed. Around the one-year mark, a full check-up visit with written notes for the next phase of growth. Your garden — and you — continue to evolve together.
