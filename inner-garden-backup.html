For private clients

Inner Garden

"For those who seek a garden that reflects not just taste, but self."

01

Deep listening session

We begin by understanding not just what you want, but who you are. Your rhythms, your sensory preferences, the life you're growing into. This isn't a 30-minute consultation — it's a 3-month dialogue where we map your inner landscape through conversations, site visits, and sensory exercises.

02

Artistic direction

Drawing from art, architecture, and the language of living plants, we craft a visual world uniquely yours. You'll receive moodboards, material palettes, and hand-drawn sketches that capture the essence of your future garden — not just how it looks, but how it feels.

03

Living composition

Every plant is chosen for meaning, movement, and seasonal unfolding — like a painting that breathes. We design planting schemes that respond to light, evolve through the year, and create moments of beauty you'll discover again and again. You'll receive detailed plans, species lists with botanical illustrations, and 3D visualizations.

04

Artisan coordination

We work with the best. Stone masons, lighting designers, horticulturists who share our obsession with detail. Every element — from the grain of the wood to the angle of a path — is orchestrated to serve the whole. We're on site weekly, ensuring the vision translates perfectly into reality.

05

A garden that evolves with you

This is just the beginning. We stay present as your garden — and you — grow. Seasonal consultations, plant care guidance, and adjustments as your life changes. Because a truly personal garden isn't static. It's alive.
